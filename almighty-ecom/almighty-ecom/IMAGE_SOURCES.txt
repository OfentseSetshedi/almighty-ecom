Storage capacities such as 64GB, 128GB and 256GB do not change a model's
physical appearance, so listings with different storage use the same exact
model picture.

For Pro and Pro Max pairs, the correct generation's Pro-family render is used
in separate files. The Pro Max file is displayed at a larger scale to represent
the larger physical model while preserving the correct camera and body design.
